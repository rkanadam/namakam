                    Seventh Anuvāka (saptamo'nuvākaḥ)

                         ———

      Homage to Dundubhya and to Āhananya; homage
      to Ghṛṣṇave and to Pramṛśa; homage to Dūta and to
      Prahita; homage to Niṣaṅgiṇe and to Iṣudhimate;
      homage to Tīkṣṇeṣave and to Āyudhine; homage to Svāyu-
      dha and to Sudhanvane; homage to Srutyā and to
      Pathyā; homage to Kāśya and to Nīpya;
      homage to Sūdya and to Sarasya; homage to Nā-
      dya and to Vaiśanta; homage to Kūpya and to
      Avaṭya; homage to Varṣya and to Avarṣya;
      homage to Meghya and to Vidyutya; homage to
      Īdhriya and to Ātapya; homage to Vātya and to
      Rebhya; homage to Vāstavya and to
      Vāstupa.
