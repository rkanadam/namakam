44                    SRI RUDRA BHASHYAM

— [the supremacy of Shri Rudra] in being the remover of all sins, in being
the bestower of the results of all modes of worship, and in bestowing through
recitation (japa) the knowledge that is the fruit of the path of knowledge — [thus
demonstrating] that [Shri Rudra is] the greatest of all the Vedas, comprising
within itself the paths of ritual action (karma-kanda), devotion (upasana-kanda),
and knowledge (jnana-kanda). And therein, all the procedures for [Rudra's]
propitiation have been set forth in accordance with the purport of the Shruti
(Vedic revelation), Smriti (recollected tradition), Itihasa (epics), and Purana
(mythological texts).

Now we shall explain the meaning of the Shri Rudra Upanishad.
