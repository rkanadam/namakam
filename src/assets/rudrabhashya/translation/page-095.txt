---
                    Fourth Anuvaka
---

[Mantra 1:]
Obeisance to the arrow-shooting women (avyadhinis), to the quivering ones — obeisance to you all.

Now the fourth anuvaka: As in the third, here too in the fourth the Lord is saluted as being the self of all (sarvatmaka). Therein the first [mantra is]: — "Nama avyadhinibhyah" (Obeisance to the arrow-shooting women). "Avyadhinyah" means women who are capable of shooting (vyadha) in all directions (a-samanta); "vivibhyantyah" means women capable of shooting in various (vividha) directions; all who exist in the world in such forms — obeisance to you (vah) — as before.

[Mantra 2:]
Obeisance to the excellent groups (uganas), to the fierce ones (trinhatih) — obeisance to you all.

Now the second [commentary]: — "Nama uganabhyah" (Obeisance to the excellent groups). "Uganah" means those of an excellent group (utkrishta-gana) — the seven divine mothers (sapta-matrika) and similar groups; and "trinhatyah" means those [goddesses] who are fierce (durgandha) and capable of causing injury — the Vamadevis and similar [divine powers]; obeisance to such forms — as before.

[Mantra 3:]
Obeisance to the greedy ones (gritsas), to the lords of the greedy — obeisance to you all.
