Nor harm our own bodies (tanucha). This fifth mantra is a prayer for the protection of all family members — young and old, male and female — from affliction, injury and death. The Lord who is Rudra, the remover of all suffering, is petitioned not to bring any harm, whether through disease or destruction, to any among our dear ones. The prayer extends from the eldest to the youngest, from parents to children, encompassing all that is near and precious to the devotee. Such is the meaning intended by this mantra.
