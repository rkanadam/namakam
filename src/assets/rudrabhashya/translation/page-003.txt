OM

|| Sri Rudra Bhashyam ||
(Commentary on the Sri Rudra Hymn)

INTRODUCTION (Upoddhatah)

May Shiva be victorious — the Lord of all knowledge (Ishana sarvavidyanam),
the Ruler of all souls, the possessor of unbroken omniscience,
the Guru of the universe, the remover of all afflictions. || 1 ||

I expound the Shataraudriya — the section of the Yajurveda —
so that a thorough and complete commentary on the Rudra hymns
may be established, drawing out its essence. || 2 ||

This work is dedicated as a humble service
to those devotees who wear sacred ash as their armor,
who bear garlands of Rudraksha beads,
whose heads are crowned with matted locks
and who stand at the four cardinal directions,
whose hearts are purified by the daily recitation of the Shataraudriya (the Shri Rudra),
and who are praised by the lord of the immortals (Indra). || 3 ||
