68                    Śrī Rudra Bhāṣyam

---

    "Salutation to you, O Blessed Lord — to Viśveśvara (the lord of all), to Mahādeva, to Svayambhu (self-born), to the destroyer of the three citadels (Tripurāntaka), to the fire at the end of time (kāśikāgnikāla), to Kālāgnīrudra, to the blue-throated one (Nīlakaṇṭha), to the conqueror of death (Mṛtyuṃjaya), to Sarveśvara (the lord of all), to Sadāśiva (the eternally auspicious), to the glorious Mahādeva — salutation."

                  Thus ends the First Anuvāka (chapter).
