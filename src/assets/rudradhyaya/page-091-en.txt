78 Sayanacharya—along with Bhatta Bhaskara Bhashya— [Na. Na. A.]

Salutations to the one in deep waters (Hridayyaya) and to the one in the dew (Niveshpyaya). || 7 ||

Sayanacharya Bhashya: 'Hridayyah' is He who exists in 'hrida', i.e., deep, unfathomable waters. 'Niveshpah' is dew-water (nihara-jala); He who exists in that is 'Niveshpyah'. || 7 ||

Bhatta Bhaskara Bhashya: Salutations to 'Hridayyaya'. To the one in the deep pool (hrida). To 'Niveshpyaya'. 'Niveshpyam' is water found in snow or frost. Salutations to the one who exists in that. || 7 ||

Salutations to the one in the fine dust (Pansavyaya) and to the one in the visible dust (Rajasyaya). || 8 ||

Sayanacharya Bhashya: 'Pansavyah' is He who is situated in 'pansu', i.e., atoms or fine dust. 'Rajasyah' is He who is situated in 'rajas', i.e., clearly visible dust. || 8 ||

Bhatta Bhaskara Bhashya: Salutations to 'Pansavyaya'. To the one in 'pansu', the subtle dust particles. To 'Rajasyaya'. To the one in the opposite, i.e., coarse dust particles. Or the word 'rajas' refers to the qualities (gunas) or the worlds (loka), as in "Yadasya pare rajasah". || 8 ||

Salutations to the one in the dry wood (Shushkyaya) and to the one in the green wood (Harityaya). || 9 ||

Sayanacharya Bhashya: 'Shushkyah' is He who exists in dry wood (shushka-kashta). 'Harityah' is He who exists in green, moist wood. || 9 ||

Bhatta Bhaskara Bhashya: Salutations to 'Shushkyaya'. 'Shushkam' is juiceless wood; to the one who is in that. To 'Harityaya'. 'Haritam' is green/moist wood; to the one who is in that. || 9 ||

Salutations to the one in the hard ground (Lopyaya) and to the one in the grass (Ulapyaya). || 10 ||

Sayanacharya Bhashya: 'Lopa' is a hard region where grass etc. is missing; He who is in that is 'Lopyah'. 'Ulapa' refers to grasses like balvaja; He who is in that is 'Ulapyah'. || 10 ||

Bhatta Bhaskara Bhashya: Salutations to 'Lopyaya'. 'Lopa' is a hard region where grass is broken or destroyed; to the one who is in that. To 'Ulapyaya'. 'Ulapa' are grasses like balvaja; to the one who is in that. Or to the one in a land containing them. || 10 ||
