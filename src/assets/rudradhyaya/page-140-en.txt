[Rudradhyaya Rituals Continued]

(Continuation of Shaunaka/Sanatkumara methods):
"The learned man who chants the Shatarudriya in the morning, noon, and evening with a pure mind, attains the fruit of all sacrifices. He is freed from all sins and becomes one with Rudra. The king who performs this ritual annually with eleven Brahmins, as described, attains universal sovereignty and his kingdom is free from all troubles."

(The text seems to end here or transition to concluding remarks).
Thus ends the Bhashya on the Rudradhyaya.
Salutations to the Supreme Soul.
Om Tat Sat.
