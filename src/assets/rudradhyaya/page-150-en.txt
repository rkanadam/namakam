[Cha. Ru. A.] Rudradhyaya | 137

Sayanacharya Bhashya: 'Sham' is a word for happiness in this world. 'Mayah' is for happiness in the next world. 'Priyam' is an object that causes affection. 'Anukamah' is an object desired due to a favorable cause. These two are worldly but differ in degree. 'Kamah' is otherworldly (heavenly) desire. 'Saumanasah' is the relatives who cause mental peace. 'Bhadram' is what is beautiful and auspicious in this world. 'Shreyah' is the benefit in the next world. 'Vasyah' is the home etc. which is the cause of residence. 'Yashah' is fame. 'Bhagah' is good fortune. 'Dravinam' is wealth. 'Yanta' is the regulator like an Acharya. 'Dharta' is the nourisher like a father. 'Kshemah' is the power to protect existing wealth. 'Dhritih' is steadiness even in calamity. 'Vishvam' is the favor... ||
