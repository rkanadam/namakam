96 Sayanacharya—along with Bhatta Bhaskara Bhashya— [Na. Da. A.]

(Bhatta Bhaskara continued). || 5 ||

[Purashcharana for this Mantra]: Having fasted for three days, one should chant the mantra 5,000 times to complete the Purashcharana. This should be used for the protection of one's own people. Sacrifice 10,000 offerings of barley and sesame smeared with ghee. Sacrifice 10,000 Udumbara sticks. Sacrifice 10,000 offerings of ghee. Every year, sacrifice 10,000 offerings of ghee. By doing this, there will be peace for one's women, children, and elderly. For other pacification rituals, chant 10,000 times. Especially, a king should have 10,000 offerings of ghee made every month for brilliance; by this, the king's inner apartments etc. also flourish. Meditation:

"Meditate on the deity with a crescent moon on His crown, with a body like the morning sun, in the form of Nandishvara, surrounded by the Lords of Ganas."

He states the sixth Rik:

Do not harm our children or grandchildren, nor our life, nor our cows, nor our horses. O Rudra, do not kill our heroes in your anger. We serve you with salutations, bringing offerings. || 6 ||

Sayanacharya Bhashya: O Rudra, do not harm ('ma ririshah') our children (tokam) or grandchildren (tanaye). Do not harm our life (ayushi). Do not harm our cows (goshu). Do not harm our horses (ashveshu). Do not kill our heroes (virah/servants) being angry ('bhamitah'). We, bringing offerings (havishmantah), serve you with salutations ('namasa'). || 6 ||
