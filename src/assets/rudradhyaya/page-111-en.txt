98 Sayanacharya—along with Bhatta Bhaskara Bhashya— [Na. Da. A.]

(Purashcharana for Mantra 6 continued): Perform the pacification ritual (Shanti) in months with favorable stars (Saumya Nakshatra) for things like horses etc. Sacrifice 10,000 Ashvattha and Apamarga sticks smeared with curd, honey, and ghee. One whose life is ebbing away gets his life restored. In gambling or disputes, one who chants this mantra is never defeated. Fast for three nights and chant continuously. Then, eating only Charu, sacrifice 10,000 Bilva fruits smeared with honey; one obtains 1,000 gold pieces. Sacrifice 10,000 lilies (kumuda) smeared with curd, honey, and ghee; one obtains a daughter. Living on Charu for a year, sacrifice 8,000 or 108 offerings of cow-ghee daily in kindled fire; one is freed from all great sins. Enchant sandalwood powder with this mantra and wear it on the forehead; everyone becomes submissive. Sacrifice 8,000 Apamarga sticks on every birthday; one conquers untimely death. One desiring rain should worship Surya with hibiscus flowers, wearing red clothes and anointing himself with red paste on a Sunday; the Lord Sun will send great rain. One traveling a long distance should take ten pieces of sugar, enchant them 8,000 times, and throw them in the ten directions; he will be safe throughout the journey. One desiring no obstacles in work should chant the mantra 10,000 times before starting; he will complete the work without effort. One desiring a son should sacrifice 100,000 offerings of ghee; he obtains a son who continues the lineage. To defeat anyone, make their form out of sand, stand on its head, and chant 10,000 times; one will certainly be victorious. Everything else is also possible through this. The power of the mantra bears fruit for those directly perfected by the Lord's grace. Meditation:

"One should always meditate on Rudra, whose body is divided eleven-fold, brilliant like pure crystal, a treasure of light, holding a trident, and having the moon on His head."

He states the seventh Rik:

Let your fierce form which kills cattle and men be far away. Let your auspicious/happiness-giving form be with us. O God, protect us, speak for us, and grant us peace in both worlds. || 7 ||
