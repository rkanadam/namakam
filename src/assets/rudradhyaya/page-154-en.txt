142 Sayanacharya—along with Bhatta Bhaskara Bhashya— [Cha. Sa. A.]

He states the seventh Anuvaka:

May the Amshu cup, the Rashmi cup, the Adabhya cup, the Adhipati cup, the Upamshu cup, the Antaryama cup, the Aindravayava cup, the Maitravaruna cup, the Ashvina cup, the Pratiprasthana cup, the Shuka cup, the Manthi cup, the Agrayana cup, the Vaishvadeva cup, the Dhruva cup, the Vaishvanara cup, the Ritu cups, the Atigrahya cups, the Aindragna cup, the Vaishvadeva cup, the Marutvatiya cups, the Mahendra cup, the Aditya cup, the Savitra cup, the Sarasvata cup, the Paushna cup, the Patnivata cup, the Hariyojana cup—may all these be mine through sacrifice. || 7 ||
