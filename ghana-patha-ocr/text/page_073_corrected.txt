stuhi śrutaṅ gartasadam̐ ...asman nivapantu sēnā:
10.8 O Rudra, scourge of the evil and the wicked, praise and
cherish the eminent young man of action seated in the lotus of
our heart, fearful as a Lion, destroyer of enemies and illustrious
of merit. Be kind and gracious to the admirer and worshipper,
who sings in praise of you. Let your forces throw off from us -
who are ungrateful and negative.

pariṇō rudrasya hētir ... tanayāya mṛḍaya
10.9 Let the stroke of fatal illness stay far from us. Let the
serious error of judgement of the brilliant specialist be far from
us. Remove the chronic diseases from the people of wealth and
power. O potent and generous lord of health and power
(Rudra), be kind and gracious to our children and our grand
children.

mīḍhuṣṭama śivatama ... pinākaṁ bibhra dāgahi
10.10 O Rudra; supreme showerer of blessings, supreme
auspicious one. Be auspicious and benevolent and bear good
mind on us. Please keep your threatening weapons on some tall
and distant trees. Come to us by wearing tiger hide garment.
Please bring your bow (Pinaaka) along with you, as an
ornament.

vikirida vilōhita namastē ... asman nivapantu tā:
10.11 O Rudra, showerer of riches, looking red. O Bhagavan,
our salutations be with you. Let your thousands of weapons
destroy our enemies, who are different from us.

sahasrāṇi sahasradhā ... parā cīnā mukhā kṛdhi
