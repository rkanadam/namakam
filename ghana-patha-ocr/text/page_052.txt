śivēna vacasātvā ...sumanā - asat
1.5 We praise and sing about Lord Rudra, who lives on the top
of the mountain. O Lord, please protect our relatives and cows
and grant them life, free of any disease. Make them live with
love towards each other,

adhyavōca dadhivaktā ...yātu dhānya:
1.6 Lord Rudra is first among everything, God of Gods, Chief
of all Doctors. He praises good deeds and neglecting the ill
deeds done by His devotees. Let him kill the animals and
people who trouble us and protect us from all evil things.

asauyas tāmrō - aruṇa ... hēḍa īmahē
1.7 He is red in colour, and becoming more red. He is golden
and praises good things. He is Sun, who is Rudra. We bow
before the thousands of Rudras, who spreads in all directions.
We request them to cool themselves.

asau yō vasarpati ... sadṛṣṭō mṛḍayāti na:
1.8 Lord Rudra has the blue neck and rises as the copper-
coloured Sun. Even the cowherds and maids who carry water
from the rivers see him as the Sun. Let this Rudra who is seen
in the form of sun, grant us all happiness.

namō - astu nīlagrīvāya ...tēbhyō karannama :
1.9 Let my salutation go; to the God who has a blue neck, to
the God who has thousands of eyes, to the God who grants us
all boons, to his devotees who are His servants.
