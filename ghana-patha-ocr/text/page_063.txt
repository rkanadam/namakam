lxi
Sixth Anuvāka

namō jyēṣṭhāya ca kaniṣṭhāya ca
6.1 Salutations to Him who is elder and who is younger.

nama: pūrvajāya cāparajāya ca
6.2 Salutations to Him who was born before all and who will
be born after all.

namō madhyamāya cāpagalbhāya ca
6.3 Salutations to Him, who is middle aged and who appears
undeveloped.

namō jaghanyāya ca budhniyāya ca
6.4 Salutations to Him, who is born from the back or end side
and from the roots.

namas sōbhyāya ca pratisaryāya ca
6.5 Salutations to Him who is born in earth and who is born in
other worlds.

namō yāmyāya ca kṣēmyāya ca
6.6 Salutations to Him, who is in the world of Yama and in the
world of safety.

nama urvaryāya ca khalyāya ca
6.7 Salutations to Him who is in the bountiful fields and in
gardens.

namaś ślōkyāya cāvasānyāya ca
6.8 Salutations to Him, who is praised in the Vedas and who is
expounded in the Vedantic Upanishads.
