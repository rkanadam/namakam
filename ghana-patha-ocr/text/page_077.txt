ōnnamō bhagavatē rudrāya viṣṇavē mr̥tyurmē pāhi
7. Om, my salutation to the omnipresent God Rudra. Protect
me from death. You Rudra, who lives in the junction of the
soul and senses, I request you to destroy everything within me
and get satisfied with my food, I take now, as offering.

ōm śāntiś śāntiś śānti:
Let there be peace, let there be peace, let there be peace.
