namō rathibhyō rathēbhyaśca vō nama:
4.8 Salutations and salutations, to the God, who ride in
chariots, and who does not have a chariot, but walk on foot.

namō rathē bhyō rathapatibhyaśca vō nama:
4.9 Salutations and salutations, to the God, who is in the form
of chariot, and their owner of the chariot.

namas sēnā bhyas sēnānibhyaśca vō nama:
4.10 Salutations and salutations, to the God, who is the soldier,
and is the leader of the armies.

nama: - kṣattṛbhyas saṅgra hītṛbhyaśca vō nama:
4.11 Salutations and salutations, to the God, who teach the
driving of chariots to others and who can drive the vehicles
themselves.

namas takṣabhyō rathakārēbhyaśca vō nama:
4.12 Salutations and salutations, to the God, who is a carpenter
and who makes chariots.

nama: kulālēbhya: karmārē bhyaśca vō nama:
4.13 Salutations and salutations, to the God, who is the potter,
and those working in metals like goldsmith, silversmiths, or
blacksmith.

nama: puñjiṣṭē bhyō niṣādēbhyaśca vō nama:
4.14 Salutations and salutations, to the God, who is the hunter
of birds who uses nets, and the fisherman uses net.

nama iṣukṛdbhyō dhanva kṛdbhyaśca vō nama:
4.15 Salutations and salutations, to the God, who makes arrows
and bows.
