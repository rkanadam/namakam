lvii
Fourth Anuvāka

nama āvyādhinī bhyō vividhyantībhyaśca vō nama
4.1 Salutations and salutations, to the God, who can hit and
pierce from all sides. and can pierce in diverse and manyfold
ways.

nama ugaṇābhyas tṛँhatībhyaśca vō nama:
4.2 Salutations and salutations, to the God, who is the form of
good spirited female Gods that help, and who is the evil
spirited revengeful and harmful Goddesses.

namō gṛtsēbhyō gṛtsapatibhyaśca vō nama:
4.3 Salutations and salutations, to the God, who is greedy and
covetous and to the chief of those who are greedy.

namō vrātē bhyō vrātapatibhyaśca vō nama:
4.4 Salutations and salutations, to the God, who is the motley
bunch of people and races, and who is the chief of such.

namō gaṇēbhyō gaṇapatibhyaśca vō nama:
4.5 Salutations and salutations, to Rudra, who is the member of
a Ganā and the is the chief of that Ganā (clan) like Vigneswara
and Nandi.

namō virūpēbhyō viśvarūpēbhyaśca vō nama:
4.6 Salutations and salutations, to the God, who looks ugly and
monstrous forms and who assume other diverse forms.

namō mahadbhya: - kṣullakēbhyaśca vō nama:
4.7 Salutations and salutations, to the God, who is a great soul,
and is a weak soul.
