1. We salute and respect Him, who is naturally scented, who
looks after His devotees by mercy, who has three eyes, O
Rudra, release us away from the catch of death, like a ripe
cucumber from its stalk, but firmly put us in the path of
salvation.

yō rudrō - agnau yō ...rudrāya namō - astu
2. We offer our salutation to that Rudra, who is in fire, water,
vegetations and pervaded in everything in this world.

tamuṣṭuhi yasviṣus ... dēvamasuran duvasya
3. We Pray and salute Him alone, who has the best arrows and
bows, who is the source and repository of all medicines to cure
all types of diseases in this world and kills Asuras. To gain the
favour and goodwill of that supreme and effulgent God Rudra,
let us worship Him, honour and adore him by our salutations.

ayaṁ mē hastō ... śivābhi marśana:
4. Since I am touching Shiva Linga with my right hand, this
hand is fortunate. No, it is relatively more fortunate than the
hands of other men. Indeed, this hand of mine is a panacea
(medicine/ solution) for all human beings for all sickness.

yē tē sahasramayutaṁ pāśā ... sarvā nava
yajāmahē
5. The God, who destroys the world, O Rudra, we request you
to keep away from us, the thousands of ropes that you have, by
which you destroy all mortal creatures. With our efficient
worship and prayers on you, we will loosen them.

mr̥tyavē svāhā mr̥tyavē svāhā
6. This offering of fire is offered to death. We offer this food in
sacrifice to Rudra, the Destroyer.
