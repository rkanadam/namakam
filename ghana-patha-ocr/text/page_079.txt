By learning Vedās, one will be able to discriminate between
the permanent and the impermanent. Behold the Self in all
beings, in all objects. To realise, that the names and forms are
illusory. Fix the mind on the Self when at work. This is the
essence of the teachings of the Vedās. Put these things in
practice in the daily battle of life. You will shine as a dynamic
person / Yogi or even a Jeevanmukta. There is no doubt about
this. Good Luck.
