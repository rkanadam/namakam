Fifth Anuvāka

namō bhavāya ca rudrāya ca
5.1 Salutations to Him who creates everything and to Him who
dispels sorrows.

namaś śarvāya ca paśupatayē ca
5.2 Salutations to Him who destroys sin and to Him who is the
Protector of all beings in bondage.

namō nīlagrīvāya ca śitikaṇṭhāya ca
5.3 Salutations to Him who has a blue neck and has white
throat.

nama: kapardinē ca vyuptakēśāya ca
5.4 Salutations to Him, who has a crown of hair and to Him
who has a shaved head.

namas sahasrākṣāya ca śatadhanvanē ca
5.5 Salutations to Him who has thousands of eyes and has
hundreds of bows.

namō giriśāya ca śipiviṣṭāya ca
5.6 Salutations to Him, who lives on the mountain and who is
in the form of Vishnu.
