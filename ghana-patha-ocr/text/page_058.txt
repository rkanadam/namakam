3.10 Salutations and salutations, to the God, who ties the string
to the bow, and who places the arrow on the string.

nama āyacchadbhyō visṛjadbhyaśca vō nama:
3.11 Salutations and salutations, to the God, who draws the
string, and who sends the arrows.

namō syadbhyō vidhyadbhyaśca vō nama:
3.12 Salutations and salutations, to the God, who shoots arrows
at the target, and who pierce the target by the arrows.

nama āsīnēbhyaś śayānēbhyaśca vō nama:
3.13 Salutations and salutations, to the Lord, who sits and who
lies down.

nama svapadbhyō jāgradbhyaśca vō nama:
3.14 Salutations and salutations, to the God, who sleeps and is
awake.

namas tiṣṭhadbhyō dhāvadbhyaśca vō nama:
3.15 Salutations and salutations, to the God, who stands still
and runs.

namas sabhābhyas sabhā patibhyaśca vō nama:
3.16 Salutations and salutations, to Rudra, who is one among
the audience, and presides over the assemblies.

namō - aśvēbhyō śvapatibhyaśca vō nama:
3.17 Salutations and salutations, to Rudra, who is the horse,
and is the Lord of the horse.
