8.2 Salutations to Him who is copper coloured (red) of the
dawn and rosy red in colour, after sunrise.

namaś śaṅgāya ca paśupatayē ca
8.3 Salutations to Him who adds pleasure to us and the Lord of
all creatures.

nama ugrāya ca bhīmāya ca
8.4 Salutations to Him who is angry and fearful at sight to His
enemies.

namō - agrēvadhāya ca dūrēvadhāya ca
8.5 Salutations to Him, who kills his enemies in front and from
a distance.

namō hantrē ca hanīyasē ca
8.6 Salutations to Him who kills his enemies and who destroys
everything at the time of Pralaya.

namō vr̥kṣēbhyō harikēśēbhyō
8.7 Salutations to Him who is the stately trees with green
leaves.

namas tārāya
8.8 Salutations to Him, who is in the form of Pranava Om.

namaś śambhavē ca mayōbhavē ca
8.9 Salutations to Him who is the source of happiness here and
pleasure of salvation.

namaś śaṅkarāya ca mayaskarāya ca
