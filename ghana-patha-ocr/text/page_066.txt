7.10 Salutations to Him, who is in the flowing waters of rivers
and in the water of the pond.

nama: kūpyāya cāvaṭyāya ca
7.11 Salutations to Him, who is in the water of wells and is in
the water of pits.

namō varṣyāya cā varṣyāya ca
7.12 Salutations to Him, who is in rainwater and in the absence
of rains.

namō mēghyāya ca vidyutyāya ca
7.13 Salutations to Him who is in the clouds and in lightning.

nama īdhriyāya cātapyāya ca
7.14 Salutations to Him, who is in the form of clear sky of
autumn and is in rain mixed with sunshine.

namō vātyāya ca rēṣmiyāya ca
7.15 Salutations to Him, who is in the form of rainy storm and
in the form of rains with hail.

namō vāstavyāya ca vāstu pāya ca
7.16 Salutations to Him who is the household wealth and the
Vaasthu Purusha guarding the house.

Eighth Anuvaka

namas sōmāya ca rudrāya ca
8.1 Salutations to Him, who is with the consort of Uma and
Lord Rudra, who removes sorrow.
