mr̥ḍā nō rudrō tanō ... tava rudra praṇītau
10.4 Rudra, be kind to us, be good and give us peace, comfort,
and good health. We respect you, (the leader and protector of
the brave) with oblations and salutations. The peace and
freedom which Manu, (the father of humanity, procured and
secured for us) we pray you, to attain under your kind
guidance.

mānō mahānta muta ...tanuvō rudra rīriṣa:
10.5 O Rudra; Lord of mercy and justice; Don't disturb our
great men, saints, scholars, and our seniors. Do not strike our
children. Do not slay our youth. Do not kill the fetus in the
womb. Do not kill our father and mother. Do not hurt our
lovely bodies and our beloved wives.

mā nastōkē tanayē ... namasā vidhēmatē
10.6 O Rudra; Lord of mercy and justice, do not strike our
children. Don't make our life short. Don't kill our cows and
horses. If you ever feel angry for our sins; please forgive us.
We pray you, not to strike our brave. With our folded hands
raised in prayer with holy offerings, we always invoke you and
pray for kindness and grace.
