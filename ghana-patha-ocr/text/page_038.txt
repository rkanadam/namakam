Veda Upangas (secondary organs of the Vedas):

1.      Mimamsa, deals with the action and knowledge based
        on interpretations of the Vedic texts
2.      Nyaya, deals with logic
3.      Purāna deal with mythology and serve as a magnifying
        glass of the Vedic Injunctions, and
4.      Dharma Sāstra deals with the Vedic codes of conduct.

These fourteen texts (Veda-4, Vedāṅga - 6 and Veda Upāṅga
- 4) are glorified as vidyasthanas - the house of true knowledge
and wisdom.

All the Vedas provide the same knowledge to experience the
Supreme through different paths.

a.      The ṛig Vedi would approach this goal through prayer
        and intellectual pursuits.
b.      The Yajur Vedi through Yajñā and invocation of Agni
        to carry the message of the hymns to the Supreme.
c.      The Sāma Vedi through musical renderings of the
        hymns
d.      The Atharva Vedi through tantra or other rituals.
