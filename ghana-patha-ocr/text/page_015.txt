What is new in the Revised Edition.

In this revised edition of Sri Rudra Ghana Patha, the meaning
in English for Sri Rudram is included. Further, Rudra Krama
Patha is also included in this book. There were a few errors
found in the previous edition which have been rectified, and
additional letters included (for dwitva) are removed.
