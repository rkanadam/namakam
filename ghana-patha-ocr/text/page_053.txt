pramuñca dhanva nastva ...parātā bhagavō vapa
1.10 Please untie the string connecting the ends of your bow, O
God, please put away arrows in your hand from out sight.

avatatya dhanus ... śivōnas sumanā bhava
1.11 O God with thousand eyes and with hundreds of Bows.
Please break the sharp ends of your arrows and loosen the
string of your Bow. Please assume/ become your auspicious
and peaceful Siva form, who do good to us.

vijyan dhanu: kapardinō .. ābhu rasya niṣaṅ gathi:
1.12 O Lord, with the mane of hair; Let your bow loosen its
string, let your quiver become empty, let your arrows lose the
power to hurt, let your sword be always in your scabbard.

yā tē hētir mīḍhuṣṭama ...a yakṣmayā paribbhuja
1.13 He who is the greatest among those who fulfil wishes of
devotees, and those weapons that He has and the bow in His
hand, do not cause infirmity to anyone, O Lord Rudra, please
save us always from all troubles.

namastē - astvāyu dhāyā ... tava dhanvanē
1.14 My salutations to your unoffensive strong weapons which
are about to strike. My salutations to your both hands and bow,

paritē dhanvanō hēti ... asman nidhē hitam
1.15 O Lord, let your arrows pass away from us, and let your
quiver be kept for our enemies,

Great Salutations to Rudra
namastē - astu bhagavan viśvēśvarāya ... śrīman
