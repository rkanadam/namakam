Third Anuvāka

namas sahamānāya nivyādhina āvyādhinīnāṁ
patayē nama:
3.1 Salutations and salutations, to the God, who suppresses his
enemies, who beats his enemies, and who is the leader of those
who beat their enemies.

nama: kakubhāya niṣaṅgiṇē stēnānāṁ patayē
nama:
3.2 Salutations and salutations, to the God, who is very
prominent, who holds the sword, and who is the leader of the
thieves.

namō niṣaṅgiṇa iṣudhimatē taskarāṇāṁ patayē
nama:
3.3 Salutations and salutations, to the God, who holds bows
ready to shoot, who has the quiver in His back, and who is the
leader of the dacoits.
