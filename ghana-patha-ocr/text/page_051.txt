Meaning of Rudram (Namakam)

The summarised meaning of Rudram in English is given
below.

ōnnamō bhagavatē rudrāya
Om Salutations to the God Rudra.

First Anuvāka

namastē rudra manyava ... bāhubhyā mutatē
nama:
1.1 Salutations to Lord Rudra, your Anger, and also salutations
to your Bow and Arrows. Salutations to your arms.

yāta iṣuś śivatamā ... tayānō rudra mṛḍaya
1.2 O Lord, Bless us with happiness. Your Arrow is Holy.
Your Bow is good. The vibration is sweet.

yātē rudra śivā ... giriśan tābhi cākaśīhi
1.3 O Rudra, you pour happiness on us from the Mountain.
You are the giver of good always. Your aspect is peaceful,
deprived of sin, path to salvation, takes us to newer heights and
reveals the principle of life.
