yō rudro - agnau yo - apsuya oṣadhīṣu yo rudro viśvā
bhuvanā viveśa tasmai rudrāya namo - astu

We bow to Sri Rudrā who is present in fire, in water, in trees
and plants and pervaded in the entire universe.
