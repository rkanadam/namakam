lxiii
namō dhr̥ṣṇavē ca pramr̥śāya ca
7.2 Salutations to Him who never runs away from war and
examines the spy information about the enemy.

namō dūtāya ca prahitāya ca
7.3 Salutations to Him, who is the messenger and the agent
sent for special purposes.

namō niṣaṅgiṇē cēṣudhimatē ca
7.4 Salutations to Him, who has the sword and has the quiver
of arrows.

nama stīkṣṇēṣavē cāyudhinē ca
7.5 Salutations to Him, who has sharp arrows and has other
weapons.

nama: svāyudhāya ca sudhanvanē ca
7.6 Salutations to Him, who has best weapons and has good
bow.

namas srutyāya ca pathyāya ca
7.7 Salutations to Him who walks in narrow footpaths and who
travels in wide roads.

nama: kāṭyāya ca nīpyāya ca
7.8 Salutations to Him, who is in canal water and who is in big
waterfalls.

namas sūdyāya ca sarasyāya ca
7.9 Salutations to Him, who is in muddy water pool and in the
lakes.

namō nādyāya ca vaiśantāya ca
