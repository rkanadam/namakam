2.5 Salutations and salutations, to the God, who has black hair,
who wears the sacred thread, and who is the lord of all those
who are healthy.

namō bhavasya hētyai jagatāṁ patayē nama:
2.6 Salutations and salutations, to the God who cuts the tree of
mortal life, and who is the lord of the Universe.

namō rudrāyātatāvinē kṣētrāṇāṁ patayē nama:
2.7 Salutations and salutations, to the God, who is Rudra, who
holds a tied bow, and who is the Lord of fields.

namas sūtāyā hantyāya vanānāṁ patayē nama:
2.8 Salutations and salutations, to the God, who is the driver of
the chariot of life, who cannot be harmed, and who is the lord
of the forest.

namō rōhitāyas thapatayē vṛkṣāṇāṁ patayē nama:
2.9 Salutations and salutations, to the God, who is red in
colour, who is protector of every thing, and who is the lord of
all trees.

namō mantriṇē vāṇijāya kakṣaṇāṁ patayē nama:
2.10 Salutations and salutations, to the Lord, who is the
minister, who is the merchant, and who is the lord of bushes
and thickets.

namō bhuvantayē vārivas kṛtā yauṣadhīnāṁ
patayē nama:
2.11 Salutations and salutations, to the God, who is the creator
of the world, who is kind to his devotees, and who is the lord of
all vegetations.
