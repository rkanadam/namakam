Tenth Anuvāka

drāpē - andhasaspatē ... ēṣāṅ kiñcanāmamat
10.1 O God, you punish the sinners in Hell, O Lord and
dispenser of food to devotees, You choose to remain poor
amidst your riches. You have a black neck and red body.
Please do not give fear to our near and dear ones or to our
cattle. Please do not give death to us and make any one sick.

yā tē rudra śivā ... tayā nō mr̥ḍa jīvasē
10.2 O, Lord Rudra; God of the world, that medicine which
cures the pain of birth and rebirth, that medicine which you
have makes us, all happy. With that gracious form (with
consort of Shakthi), please bless us all to lead a full and happy
life.
