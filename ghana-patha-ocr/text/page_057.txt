3.4 Salutations and salutations, to the God, who cheats, who is
the greatest cheat, and who is leader of those who steal by
cheating.

namō nicēravē paricarāyā raṇyānāṁ patayē nama:
3.5 Salutations and salutations, to the Lord, who goes inside to
steal, who goes outside to steal, and who is the leader of
thieves who steal in the forest.

namas sṛkāvibhyō jighāṁ sadbhyō muṣṇatāṁ
patayē nama:
3.6 Salutations and salutations, to the Lord, who defends
themselves with arms. troubles others and is the leader of
people who steal crop and wealth from their Master.

namōsimadbhyō naktañ caradbhya: prakṛntānāṁ
patayē nama:
3.7 Salutations and salutations, to the God, who holds the
sword, who hang around in the night to steal, and who is the
leader of those who murder and steal.

nama uṣṇīṣiṇē giricarāya kuluñcānāṁ patayē
nama:
3.8 Salutations and salutations, to the God, who wears the
turban, who wanders in the mountains, and who is the leader of
landgrabbers.

nama iṣumadbhyō dhanvāvibhyaśca vō nama:
3.9 Salutations and salutations, to the Lord, who holds the
Darts. and who holds the bows.
