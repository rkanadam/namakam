lxii
namō vanyāya ca kakṣyāya ca
6.9 Salutations to Him, who exists as tree in forest and to him
who exists as creepers in bushes.

nama: śravāya ca pratiśravāya ca
6.10 Salutations to Him who is of the form of sound and is in
the form of echo of the sound.

nama āśuṣēṇāya cāśurathāya ca
6.11 Salutations to Him, whose armies are the fast moving, and
who rides on a fast-moving chariot.

namaś śūrāya cāvabhindatē ca
6.12 Salutations to Him, who is in the form of warrior, who
pierce his enemies.

namō varmiṇē ca varūthinē ca
6.13 Salutations to Him, who is armoured and who provides
safety to His charioteers.

namō bilminē ca kavacinē ca
6.14 Salutations to Him, who wears the helmet and a
breastplate.

nama: śrutāya ca śrutasēnāya ca
6.15 Salutations to Him who is praised in the Vedas and has a
famed army.

Seventh Anuvāka

namō dundubhyāya cāhananyāya ca
7.1 Salutations to Him, who is of the sound of a big drum and
is in the form of the drumstick.
