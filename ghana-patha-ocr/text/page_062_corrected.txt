namō mīḍhuṣṭamāya cēṣumatē ca
5.7 Salutations to Him who gives heavy rains and who carries
arrows.

namō hrasvāya ca vāmanāya ca
5.8 Salutations to Him who is small and who is dwarf.

namō bṛhatē ca varṣīyasē ca
5.9 Salutations to Him, who is great and majestic, and who is
full of all excellences.

namō vṛddhāya ca saṁvṛdhvanē ca
5.10 Salutations to Him, who is ancient and is loudly praised
by the scriptures.

namō - agriyāya ca prathamāya ca
5.11 Salutations to Him, who existed before creation and who
is foremost.

nama āśavē cājirāya ca
5.12 Salutations to Him, who is omnipresent and who moves
fast.

namaś śīghriyāya ca śībhyāya ca
5.13 Salutations to Him, who is in fast streams and who is in
headlong cascades.

nama ūrmyāya cāvasvanyāya ca
5.14 Salutations to Him who is in fast tides and who is in still
water.

nama: srōtasyāya ca dvīpyāya ca
5.15 Salutations to Him who is in floods and who is in islands.
