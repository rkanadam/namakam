lxvii
Ninth Anuvaka

nama iriṇyāya ca prapathyāya ca
9.1 Salutations to Him who lives in saline arid who walks the
trodden paths.

nama: kiṁśilāya ca kṣayaṇāya ca
9.2 Salutations to Him who lives in uninhabitable rocky areas,
and one lives in good places.

nama: kapardinē ca pulastayē ca
9.3 Salutations to Him who has matted hair (Sikha) and stands
before His devotees to protect them.

namō gōṣṭhyāya ca gr̥hyāya ca
9.4 Salutations to Him who lives in cowshed and lives in
homes.

namas talpyāya ca gēhyāya ca
9.5 Salutations to Him who sits on the cot and who lives in
multi storeyed building.

nama: kāṭyāya ca gahvarēṣṭhāya ca
9.6 Salutations to Him who lives in thorny impenetrable forest
places and inaccessible mountain caves.

namō hradayyāya ca nivēṣpyāya ca
9.7 Salutations to Him, who is in deep waters and in dew
drops.

nama: pāṁsavyāya ca rajasyāya ca
9.8 Salutations to Him, who is in subtle dust and who is in the
visible dust.
