Eleventh Anuvāka

sahasrāṇi sahasraśō yē ...dhanvāni tanmasi
11.1 O Rudra, we request you to order your thousands of
soldiers, who are having thousand kinds of weapons, to loosen
them and keep thousands of yojanas far away from us in this
earth.

asmin mahatyarṇavē ntarikṣē bhavā - adhi
11.2 In this vast sea of interspace between heaven and earth
(Antariksha), wherever Rudra Ganā dwells.

nīlagrīvāś śiti kaṇṭhāś śarvā - adha: - kṣamācarā:
11.3 And those Rudra Ganā, who live under the earth (Paathala
Loka) Whose half side of neck is blue, and the other half is
white.

nīlagrīvāś śitikaṇṭhā divaṁ rudrā - upaśritā:
11.4 And those Rudras who live in heaven, whose half side of
neck is blue, and the other half is white.

yē vr̥kṣēṣu saspiñjarā nīlagrīvā vilōhitā:
11.5 And those Rudras who live in trees with a colour of tender
grass and red, but having blue colour neck,

yē bhūtānā madhipatayō viśikhāsa: kapardina:
11.6 And those Rudras, who are leaders of Bhoota Ganās,
some of them are bald having no hairs and some of them
wearing the matted locks.
