lxvi
8.10 Salutations to Him who is inherently of the nature of
giving the pleasure directly in this world and gives the pleasure
of the other worlds.

namaśśivāya ca śivatarāya ca
8.11 Salutations to Him; the auspicious one and who is more
auspicious than all others.

namas tīrthyāya ca kūlyāya ca
8.12 Salutations to Him who is the holy water and is
worshipped on the banks of rivers.

nama: pāryāya cāvāryāya ca
8.13 Salutations to Him who is being praised by great souls
after salvation and grants all that one wants in this world.

nama: prataraṇāya cōttaraṇāya ca
8.14 Salutations to Him, who helps us cross the river of sins
and lifts us to salvation.

nama ātāryāya cālādyāya ca
8.15 Salutations to Him who makes soul enter this world and
encourages soul (Jeeva) to enjoy the fruit of actions. (Karma)

namaś śaṣpyāya ca phēnyāya ca
8.16 Salutations to Him who is in the form of tender grass and
foam of the river.

namas sikatyāya ca pravāhyāya ca
8.17 Salutations to Him who is in the form of sand and the
running water of the river.
