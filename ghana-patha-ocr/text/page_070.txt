lxviii
namaś śuṣkyāya ca harityāya ca
9.9 Salutations to Him, who is in dried piece of wood and who
is in green stem.

namō lōpyāya cōlapyāya ca
9.10 Salutations to him who is in firm floor and who is on
lawn.

nama ūrvyāya ca sūrmyāya ca
9.11 Salutations to Him, who is on flat land and on waves in
the river.

nama : parṇyāya ca parṇaśadyāya ca
9.12 Salutations to Him who is in green leaves and is in dried
leaves.

namō paguramāṇāya cābhighnatē ca
9.13 Salutations to the Rudra Ganās, who have their weapons
uplifted and strike from the front.

nama ākkhidatē ca prakkhidatē ca
9.14 Salutations to Rudra Ganās, who troubles a little and
troubles seriously.

namō va: kirikēbhyō dēvānāṁ hr̥dayēbhya:
9.15 Salutations to Him, who showers riches on his devotees
and dwell in hearts of Devas.

namō vikṣīṇakēbhyō
9.16 Salutations to your deathless form in the hearts of Gods.

namō vicinvatkēbhyō
