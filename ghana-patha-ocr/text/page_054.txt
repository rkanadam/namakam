lii
Salutations to you, the God, who is the lord of the Universe,
who is the greatest among Gods, who has three eyes, who
destroyed three Cities, who is the master of the three Fires,
who is the Rudra who burns the World, who has a blue neck,
who won over the God of Death, who is Lord of every thing,
Who is ever peaceful, and who is the greatest God with
goodness. Salutations again.

Second Anuvāka

namō hiraṇyabāhavē sēnānyē diśāñca patayē
nama:
2.1 Salutations and salutations, To the God with golden hands,
who is the chief of armies, who is the chief of four directions.

namō vṛkṣēbhyō harikēśēbhya: paśūnāṁ patayē
nama:
2.2 Salutations and salutations, to the God, who shines as trees,
who has green leaves as hair, who is the lord of all beings.

namas saspiñjarāya tviṣimatē pathīnāṁ patayē
nama:
2.3 Salutations and salutations, to the God who is reddish
yellow like leaf buds, who shines in radiant, and one leads us in
different Paths.

namō babhlušāya vivyādhinēnnānāṁ patayē nama:
2.4 Salutations and salutations, to the God, who rides on the
bull, who is like disease to his enemies, and who is the lord of
all food.
